FAQs

How do I change my daily goal?
Open AquaBean → Settings → Daily Goal.

How do I switch units?
Settings → Units (ml/oz).

I’m not receiving reminders.
Ensure notifications are allowed for AquaBean in iOS Settings and that “Enable Nudges” is ON in the app.

Does AquaBean sync data?
Current version stores data locally on your device. Sync may come in a future build.
