AquaBean Privacy Policy

Effective: 31 Aug 2025

AquaBean is a simple daily water tracker. We designed it to be private by default.

Data We Collect

None. AquaBean does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Your hydration entries and settings remain on your device.

Third‑Party Services

AquaBean does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or external databases. If in a future version you opt into features like iCloud sync or Health integration, those will be clearly explained and optional.

Permissions

  • Notifications (optional): for hydration reminders you enable. Notification content is generated locally.
