Privacy Policy

Effective: 31 Aug 2025

Privacy by Design

AquaBean is a simple daily water tracker designed to be private by default.

Data We Collect

None.

AquaBean does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Your hydration entries and settings remain securely on your device.

Third‑Party Services

AquaBean does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or external databases. If in a future version you opt into features like iCloud sync or Health integration, those will be clearly explained and optional.

Permissions

  • Notifications (optional): for hydration reminders you enable. Notification content is generated locally on your device.

Data Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We don't have access to your personal information, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on GitHub.
